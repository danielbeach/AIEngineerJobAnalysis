AI Strategy Consultant (Senior) - Work directly with founders to turn AI into real business outcomes



The Opportunity

Most companies are experimenting with AI, but very few are doing it well. We’re working with European SMEs that don’t need hype... they need clarity, strategy, and execution. That’s where you come in.


What You’ll Actually Do

This is not a “build models in isolation” role. You will:
Sit with founders and executives and identify where AI creates real value
Lead AI audits (readiness, use cases, competitive landscape)
Turn insights into clear, actionable roadmaps
Help teams move from ideas → MVPs → real implementation
Advise on LLMs, RAG systems, and AI architecture decisions
Challenge assumptions and bring an external expert perspective


What Makes This Role Different

You’re not just building → you’re shaping decisions

You’re not working with theory → you’re solving real business problems

Is comfortable leading conversations with senior stakeholders
Has hands-on exposure to: LLMs, RAG architectures, AI product workflows

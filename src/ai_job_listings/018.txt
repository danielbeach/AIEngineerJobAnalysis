We’re hiring an AI Engineer to design and build the agent layer of our platform, enabling a member-facing AI assistant to use tools, navigate multi-step tasks, and handle complex member journeys (benefits, claims, prior auth guidance, provider search, and more). In this role you’ll define agent patterns, implement orchestration and guardrails, and partner with product and domain teams to deliver high-impact capabilities that reduce friction and improve the member experience.

To do that, you’ll need:

Experience building agentic systems (tool use, planning, routing, memory) with modern frameworks
Strong software engineering fundamentals (Python), APIs, eventing/orchestration patterns
Proven ability to implement guardrails: policy constraints, safe actions, auditability, and fallbacks

If you’re inspired by our mission – peace of mind through better health – and energized by this work, we’d love to hear from you.

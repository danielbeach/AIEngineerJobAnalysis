Role Description

As an AI / ML Engineer focused on LLMs, Agents, and Simulation Modeling, you will lead the integration of large language models into agentic workflows, simulation platforms, and real-time decision engines. This role sits at the intersection of applied AI, systems engineering, and operational decision-making. You’ll work on the bleeding edge of LLM-powered reasoning, embedding AI directly into systems that operate under uncertainty and high-stakes constraints. Your work will directly influence national security outcomes.
